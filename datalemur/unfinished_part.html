Unfinished Parts [Tesla SQL Interview Question]

Study Case

Tesla is investigating production bottlenecks and they need your help to extract the relevant data. Write a query to determine which parts have begun the assembly process but are not yet finished. Assumptions: parts_assembly table contains all parts currently in production, each at varying stages of the assembly process. An unfinished part is one that lacks a finish_date. This question is straightforward, so let's approach it with simplicity in both thinking and solution.

Example Tables

part finish_date assembly_step
battery 01/22/2022 00:00:00 1
battery 02/22/2022 00:00:00 2
battery 03/22/2022 00:00:00 3
bumper 01/22/2022 00:00:00 1
bumper 02/22/2022 00:00:00 2
bumper 3
bumper 4

Query

SELECT part, assembly_step FROM parts_assembly
WHERE finish_date ISNULL
